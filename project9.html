Flipkart - India's Leading Giant E-Commerce

Overview

This project analyzes Flipkart’s e-commerce performance by tracking customer behavior, sales patterns, and retention insights. The interactive dashboard highlights key performance indicators, custom metrics, and provides valuable business insights for decision-making.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

  • Total Sales
  • Unit Price
  • Net Sales Amount
  • Items Sold
  • Order Count
  • Average Customer Rating

Workflow

The project followed a structured process to ensure clean, consistent, and business-ready data for visualization:

  • Excel: Performed data quality checks, removed null values, and standardized inconsistencies in payment methods (e.g., “Net_Banking” → “Net Banking”).
  • Power BI Query Editor: Verified data quality, ensured 100% consistency, and transformed raw data into structured formats suitable for modeling.
  • Power BI: Created 10 custom columns:
    • Unit Price
    • Net Sales Amount
    • Discount Category
    • Average Order Value
    • Sales Category Group
    • Rating Category
    • Sales Year
    • Sales Month
    • Sales Quarter
    • Day Name
  • Designed intuitive visuals, slicers, and KPI cards for seamless user interaction.

Technology Stack

  • Excel – Data cleaning & preprocessing
  • Power BI Query Editor – Data transformation & validation
  • Power BI – Visualization, dashboard design, and KPI tracking
