📌 Overview

The Flipkart Dashboard project provides a detailed analysis of Flipkart’s sales performance and customer behavior. Using Excel for preprocessing and Power BI for analytics, the dashboard highlights sales trends, order distribution, pricing insights, and customer ratings. This interactive dashboard empowers decision-makers to evaluate KPIs and track performance effectively.

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

  • ‣ Total Sales
  • ‣ Unit Price
  • ‣ Net Sales Amount
  • ‣ Items Sold
  • ‣ Order Count
  • ‣ Average Customer Rating

⚙️ Workflow

• Excel:

  • Performed data quality checks and removed null values.
  • Cleaned inconsistencies in Payment Method (e.g., replaced “Net_Banking” with “Net Banking”).

• Power BI Query Editor:

  • Verified data quality ensuring 100% consistency.
  • Transformed raw data into structured formats for accurate modeling.

• Power BI:

  • Added 10 Custom Columns: Unit Price, Net Sales Amount, Discount Category, Average Order Value, Sales Category Group, Rating Category, Sales Year, Sales Month, Sales Quarter, Day Name.
  • Designed intuitive visuals, slicers, and KPI cards for seamless interaction.
