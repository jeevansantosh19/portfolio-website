Animal Health Monitoring and Disease Detection

Leveraging Machine Learning for Early Disease Detection in Animals

Project Overview

This project applies machine learning techniques to monitor animal health and detect diseases in livestock. By analyzing key health indicators, we enhance early disease detection and improve farm management efficiency.

  • Machine Learning Model Used: Random Forest Classifier
  • Tools: Python, Scikit-Learn, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib

Performance Summary

The Random Forest Classifier was trained to detect diseases in animals based on key health features. The model achieved an accuracy of 78.05%, demonstrating its effectiveness in veterinary diagnostics. In testing, the model predicted Pneumonia with a 69.5% probability, showcasing its ability to identify respiratory diseases. This project highlights the potential of AI-driven diagnostics in reducing risks and improving livestock health management.
