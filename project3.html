E-Commerce Sales Analysis

Analyzing Sales Trends and Customer Behavior for Better Business Insights

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing sales patterns and customer behaviors in an e-commerce platform. By leveraging data analytics tools, we extract meaningful insights to optimize sales strategies and improve business decisions.

  • Tool Used: Power BI
  • Key Metrics Analyzed: Revenue Trends, Customer Segmentation, Product Performance

Insights & Findings

Our analysis revealed key sales trends, such as seasonal demand spikes and top-performing product categories. We also identified high-value customers and their purchasing patterns, helping businesses tailor their marketing strategies.
