🍪 Kevin Cookie Company

Global Cookie Industry Leader

📌 Overview

The Kevin Cookie Company is a leading force in the global cookie industry, known for its innovative flavors, sustainable production, and strong market performance. This project provides a detailed analysis of cookie sales, profits, and revenue using Excel, Power Query, and Power BI.

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

📦 Total Units Sold

Total cookies sold across all regions.

💰 Total Profit

Overall profit generated from cookie sales.

📊 Total Revenue

Comprehensive revenue achieved globally.

📏 Avg. Revenue Per Unit

Average revenue per cookie sold.

🏷️ Total Cost

Total cost of production and operations.

📉 Profit %

Profit margin percentage across sales.

🛠️ Workflow

  • 📄 Excel – Source file for sales data.
  • 🔍 Power Query Editor – Conducted thorough data quality checks.
  • 📊 Power BI – Added 12 calculated columns and 6 DAX measures for advanced analysis and created interactive dashboards.
